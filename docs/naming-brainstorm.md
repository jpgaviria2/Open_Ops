# CounterStack Naming Brainstorm

`Open_Ops` is the repository/internal codename. The customer-facing product should use a merchant-native name.

## Recommendation

## CounterStack

**Tagline:** The operating stack behind the counter.

Why it works:

- Signals a complete stack, not a single app.
- Feels natural for cafes, retail, restaurants, and service merchants.
- Leaves room for customer apps, staff ops, rewards, inventory, payments, and AI.
- Sounds more productized than “Open Ops.”

## Other candidates

- **ShopPilot** — friendly AI/control-surface feel.
- **MerchantLoop** — loyalty + operations feedback loop.
- **OpenTill** — strong open-source/POS/Bitcoin signal, but payment-heavy.
- **MainstreetOS** — clear local-business mission, needs trademark/domain check.
- **CounterFlow** — workflow-heavy and practical.
- **IndieStack** — independent merchant energy, slightly developer-coded.
- **StorePilot** — simple and customer-friendly, but generic.
